# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a productive project:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep technical discussion focused on the issue or pull request.
- Respect copyright, likeness rights, voice rights, model licenses, and platform terms.
- Do not post private media, private voice samples, secrets, or non-redistributable model files.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that are abusive, off-topic, unsafe, or legally risky for an open-source repository.

Maintainers may temporarily or permanently restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.
